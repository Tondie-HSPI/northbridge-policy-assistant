# Business Case

Organizations often store policy and operations guidance across PDFs, spreadsheets, intake files, and internal manuals. Staff need fast answers, but they also need accuracy and consistency.

Northbridge Policy Assistant demonstrates how a controlled AI assistant can support that work.

## Business Value

- Reduces manual document search time.
- Improves consistency of first-pass answers.
- Encourages source-backed responses.
- Escalates unsupported questions to human reviewers.
- Creates a reusable pattern for AI-assisted knowledge work.

## Why It Is Portfolio-Relevant

This project shows practical AI implementation, not just prompt writing. It demonstrates document ingestion, retrieval, tool routing, confidence handling, and responsible handoff design.
